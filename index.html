What Defines Us

Our values are rooted in the 5 pillars that define our vision and drive our mission to support physicians and improve care.

Culture

A shared set of workplace beliefs, values, attitudes, standards, purposes and behaviours. It reflects both written and unwritten rules that we at C5 follow.

Compassion

An understanding of differing perspectives and show genuine empathy and care.

Competence

The quality of having sufficient knowledge, judgement, skills and the strength to provide the best possible service.

Character

The way one thinks, feels and behaves. At C5 Health, we believe in high moral character; always keeping in mind justice for all involved.

Commitment

Our pledge to put forth our best possible effort to attain desirable results with honour and respect.
